WEF Nexus

During my work experience with design studio Loop.pH, I researched issues of sustainability and communicated them visually through graphics. This included developing visualisations for communicating the concept of the Water Energy and Food Nexus. The Water, Energy and Food Nexus is a way of thinking about how these vital resources are inextricably linked and how you should consider the impact of changes to the others. I built on the excellent feedback I received and designed an animation to further help visualise the system.
