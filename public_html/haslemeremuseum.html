Haslemere Museum

Haslemere Museum is a large natural history museum in Surrey. I contributed designs for their fundraising material including the "Museum in a Million" campaign, which celebrated the museums 125th anniversary. The art direction for the brochure was based on butterflies, which are both a significant part of the museums collection and make comparisons to the museum as a rare species. I was fortunate enough to work directly with the museums professional team. The final brochure design contributed to winning a 2014 Museum + Heritage Award for best project on a limited budget.
