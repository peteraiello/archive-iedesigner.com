ICCHP Center

The ICCHP is a hypnosis and hypnotherapy teaching center in London. Through working with the director, I designed the branding for the identity and website. The theme was developed from the integrated approach to learning that the center takes. Students from different professional backgrounds and at different stages of progression learn from each other. This was demonstrated through connected circles, which could just as easily represent the interconnected nature of learning as much as it could neuron cells and brain activity. The design of the website carried this concept through, with the graphics representing the unique approach. The client was appreciative of both the design and my collaborative way of working. The website was developed using the CMS Joomla!.
